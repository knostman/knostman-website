Welcome to Ms. Knostman's Website

Here are a few of my favorite Edtech Resources:

  • GeoGebra
    • I enjoy using GeoGebra for geometric investigations. It makes creating geometric constructions easy and manipulatable.
    • There are lots of ways to use Geogebra
  • PhET Simulations
    • PhET Simulations has several free, online simulations that students can manipulate and conduct investigations with. It has both math and science simulations.
  • Wolfram Alpha
    • Wolfram Alpha is a free online resource that can both be used as a reference and as an online calculator.
  • Desmos
    • Desmos is an online graphing calculator that also allows teachers to create interactive lessons and assignments for students.
