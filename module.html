1. Which variable represents the slope in Slope Intercept Form? $$ y=mx+b $$

The \(m\) represents slope.

2. How would this Standard Form equation be written in Slope Intercept Form? $$ y - 2 = 3 ( x + 1) $$

Answer: \(y = 3 x + 5 \)
